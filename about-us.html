Why should you invest time on this website?

All around the world, people
are subjected to various inequities
based on race/skin color, disabilities,
sexual preferences and more. The
biases of the world, work to cripple
the unity of our nations, causing
irreversible damage to the lives and
communities that we share. In order to "break
the fourth wall," we have to understand and
help others to understand that we are all equal.
Our mission is to create an accepting
community, a place where anyone can feel
safe, happy, and encouraged!
